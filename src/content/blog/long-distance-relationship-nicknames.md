## The secret: don't pick from a list

Here's the honest truth about nicknames — the ones that *stick* are almost never chosen on purpose. They emerge:

- From a **mispronounced word** one of you said once.
- From an **inside joke** only you two understand.
- From a **thing they always do** ("Blanket Thief," "Snack Bandit").
- From **how you met** or a place that matters to you both.

So treat the lists above as a starting point, not the destination. The category that fits their personality gets you close; a real moment between you gets you the one that lasts.
